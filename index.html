TILES!

Do you know how to click a mouse? Outstanding! That's all you need to know to play this game. On the screen behind me, you will see an 8x8 grid of tiles. These tiles will light up randomly. The object of the game is to click on the highlighted tiles before they go dark.

You will have 20 chances to click on
ten highlighted tiles.
If you are successful, you can advance to the next level.

Each successive level is just a little bit
faster than the one before it.

Oh yeah, just sitting there watching the tiles light up and not doing anything...

that's not gonna buy you any time!

Each time a tile lights up and you don't click on it, it counts against you. Are you ready to play?
